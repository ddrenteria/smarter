Notes for the implementing engineer/AI
Statuses:

"shield_next_push_against_you" reduces the next incoming push instance to 0 (consume on trigger).

"reflect_next_push" mirrors the next received push during duration (before shield).

"randomize_next_card_effect" mutates the next card's tier actions to a random legal effect of similar magnitude.

"global_random_effects" applies the above mutation to all card effects for both players (live-ops toggleable).

Durations: -1 means until end of match.

Determinism: All randoms must draw from the server seed for replays.

Order of resolution: Opponent's recruited effect resolves first, then the remaining one; evaluate end-step after both.
